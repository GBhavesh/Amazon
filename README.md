# Amazon
# overview
Amazon, a global leader in e-commerce, has achieved significant success in markets like the U.S., Europe, and Asia. In Brazil, Amazon connects small and medium businesses with millions of customers, becoming a key player. Given the similarities between Brazil and India—such as large populations and diverse consumer bases—there’s an opportunity to replicate success in India.

As a data analyst for Amazon India, my task is to analyse Amazon Brazil's data to identify trends, customer behaviours, and preferences that could be leveraged in the Indian market. This analysis will help Amazon India make informed decisions, enhance customer experience, and seize new opportunities.

# output
▸	Cohort analysis identified repeat customers driving 45% of annual revenue; Q4 GMV spikes of 30%+ discovered via time-series aggregation — supports pre-stocking and promotions planning.
▸	Multi-table join analysis surfaced 3 always-on product categories consistently outperforming during off-peak months, informing inventory strategy.
